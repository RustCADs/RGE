working-tree EOL changed â€” so this is a checkout-only normalization with no

1. Added the three `*.rge-*` rules to `.gitattributes`.
2. Ran `git add --renormalize` over the eleven affected RGE paths to attach the

   copies still needed to be re-materialized.
