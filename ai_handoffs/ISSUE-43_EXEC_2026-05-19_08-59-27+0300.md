  untransformed origin position. It asserts `pick.entity` equals the
  Transform-root entity, `pick.owner` equals `ENTITY_OWNER`, `pick.face_id`
  equals `BRepFaceId::for_cuboid_face(ENTITY_OWNER, CuboidFaceTag::PosZ)`
  (the upstream Cuboid +Z identity, proving Transform face labels survive
  the picker path), and `pick.t ≈ 3.5` (transformed +Z face at `z = +1.5`,
  ray origin at `z = +5`).
