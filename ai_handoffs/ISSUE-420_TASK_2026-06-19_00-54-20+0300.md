## Acceptance Criteria

- `EditorShell::delete_current_cad_cuboid` submits a `DeleteCurrentCadCuboid`
  action through the existing generic CommandBus context.
- `rge-editor-actions` is untouched and remains generic: no CAD, projection,
  graph, B-Rep, or editor-shell references are introduced.
- The delete action is immutable. It captures `from_head`, the empty parent
  checkpoint to restore to on apply, the tracked entity id, and the expected
  single-root/single-node state before submit. It uses no `unsafe`, no globals,
  no static mutable state, no broad interior mutability workaround, and no
  action-trait or CommandBus signature change.
- Apply deletes the tracked single cuboid by restoring to the empty parent
  checkpoint, despawning the tracked B-Rep entity, ticking projection, clearing
  `cad_entity`, and pruning entity and face selections that reference the
  deleted entity.
- Undo restores `from_head`, respawns/projects the root as a fresh tracked
  entity, ticks projection, and does not resurrect the old entity id.
- Redo deletes again and leaves no stale `render_mesh_for` lookup, tracked
  entity, selected entity, selected face, or selected-CAD bounds.
- Empty, stale, partial, multi-root, multi-node, missing-parent, and otherwise
  non-live tracked states reject without mutating CAD/render/selection state and
  without growing the CommandBus undo stack.
- Existing add, stale-id cleanup, and inspection behavior remains covered by
  the requested filters.
- `.ai/dispatch.tasks.md` ends this feature by appending exactly one task 163
  gated audit. Task 162 must not directly record a `NEEDS_HUMAN_RECORDED`
  marker unless it cannot safely append task 163.
