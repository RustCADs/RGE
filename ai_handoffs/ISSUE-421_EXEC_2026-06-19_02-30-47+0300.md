The action implements `Action<EditorShellActionContext>` and obtains CAD mutation access through `context.cad_mutation_state()` (commands.rs:566-583).
- `rge-editor-actions` boundary: yes, it remains generic. `action.rs` defines generic `ActionContext`, `ActionContextFamily`, `WorldActionContext`, and `Action<F>` without CAD/editor-shell types, while `bus.rs` stores `CommandBus<F: ActionContextFamily>` and submits `Box<dyn Action<F>>` against `F::Context<'_>`. The manifest depends only on kernel ECS, audit-ledger, diagnostics, serde, thiserror, and tracing. The required inverse grep over `crates/editor-actions/src` and `crates/editor-actions/Cargo.toml` returned no CAD/editor-shell/render/shell-specific matches.
- Add/delete/undo/redo lifecycle: yes, the shell-owned lifecycle is coherent in the allowed evidence. Add moves the bus cursor to 1, marks dirty, installs graph/world/projection state, tracks a live entity, and exposes one render mesh; undo clears the tracked entity, returns graph head to the pre-add checkpoint, despawns the B-Rep, leaves no stale `render_mesh_for`, and moves dirty/cursor state; redo restores the original committed head with a fresh live tracked entity and render mesh (tests.rs:2475-2598). Delete records a second CommandBus action, moves the cursor to 2, restores the empty parent head, clears the tracked entity, clears live B-Rep/projection mesh counts, prunes entity and face selections, and leaves no stale CAD lookup (tests.rs:2633-2688). Undoing delete restores the original graph head but uses a fresh entity id, projects it, and still leaves no lookup for the deleted entity; redoing delete prunes the fresh entity selection and leaves no stale lookup (tests.rs:2691-2777). Redoing delete after undoing and redoing the add also succeeds with stack len 2, cursor 2, empty graph/projection/world state, selection pruning, and no stale render lookups for old or redone entities (tests.rs:2780-2862).
- Rejected invalid states: yes, rejected empty, stale, partial, and multi-node states are checked before mutation/stack growth in the test helper. The helper captures inspection state, entity selection, face selection, bus stack len, cursor, and dirty state, then asserts they are unchanged after `delete_current_cad_cuboid` returns `CadCuboidDeleteError::NotDeletable` (tests.rs:2865-2904), and the test applies it to empty, stale, partial, and multi-node shells (tests.rs:2907-2948).
