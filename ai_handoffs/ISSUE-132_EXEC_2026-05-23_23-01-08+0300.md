   `self.command_bus.submit(action, self.world.kernel_mut())`, (f) records

   `rge_kernel_ecs::World`'s `TimeScale` resource via
   `World::insert_resource(TimeScale::with_value(2.0))`. The bus's internal
   audit ledger records one `EventKind::Action` event (with `SetTimeScale::payload()`
