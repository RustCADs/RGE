  `AnimGraph` with two states (`idle`, `run`) joined by one transition
  (`start_run`), captures the old snapshot with
  `GraphSnapshot::from_graph(&g.graph)`, then mutates the same graph by
  adding exactly one new state (`jump`) and exactly one new transition
  (`run -> jump`, trigger `leap`). It captures the new snapshot, runs
  `GraphDiff::between(&old_snapshot, &new_snapshot)`, and asserts:
  - `added_nodes` has length 1 and `added_nodes[&jump]` equals
    `AnimState { key: "jump" }`;
  - `added_edges` has length 1 and `added_edges[&new_edge_id]` equals
    `EdgeRecord { src: run, dst: jump, data: AnimTransition::new("leap") }`;
  - `removed_nodes`, `removed_edges`, `changed_nodes`, and `changed_edges`
    are all empty.
