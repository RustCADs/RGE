### Tests

- `crates/cad-projection/tests/render_adapter_smoke.rs::loft_render_mesh_face_labels_align_with_projection_lookup`:
  new additive `#[test]` (Test 8). Builds the canonical square-profile →
  larger-square-profile `LoftOp` fixture (n=4, unit square to 3×3
  square, length 1.0) mirrored from `loft_brep_face_id_lookup_smoke.rs`,
  commits a `CadGraph` whose root is the Loft node, spawns the projected
  entity bound to that Loft root, and sets `BRepHandle.brep_owner =
  Some(ENTITY_OWNER)` before projection. After `projection.tick`, it
  calls both `projected_mesh(entity)` and `render_mesh_for(entity,
  &world)` and asserts:
  - the projected mesh has exactly 12 triangles (4n−4 for n=4);
  - `RenderMesh.face_labels` is `Some` with exactly 12 opaque `u64`
    labels — one per projected triangle, equal to `projected.triangle_count()`
    and to `projected_labels.len()`;
  - renderer label N equals the projected `TopologyFaceId.0` at index N
    (exact value + order, not `Some(_)`);
  - no label is `TopologyFaceId::DEGENERATE` (LoftOp mints stable
    bottom-cap, top-cap, and side labels);
  - for every triangle, wrapping the renderer `u64` as
    `TopologyFaceId(label)` and resolving it through
    `brep_face_ids_for_node(graph.graph(), loft_node, ENTITY_OWNER)`
    yields the exact `BRepFaceId` returned by
    `projection.brep_face_id_for_triangle(entity, tri, &world,
    graph.graph())` for the same triangle.
  The assertion does not accept any face, any owner, a sorted set, or a
  deduplicated set.
