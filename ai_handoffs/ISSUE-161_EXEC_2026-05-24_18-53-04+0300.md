## Open Questions for Reviewer

- Float-variant note: with `ron 0.12.1`, parsing unsuffixed floats like `1.0471976` and `100000.0` through `ron::Value` yields `Number::F64`, not `Number::F32`, even though the source intent is an `f32` literal. The new helper `expect_float` accepts either variant and widens F32 to f64 before the comparison, so the assertions compare against the f64 literals `1.047_197_6_f64` and `100_000.0_f64`. The `f32` intent is still pinned because the raw payload string is asserted byte-for-byte and the substring `"fov_y_radians: 1.0471976"` is required to appear inside `"Perspective("`. Please confirm this is the intended boundary — assert byte-shape for the f32 spelling, but inspect numeric value through Value's loose float typing — given that `rge-data` deliberately does not depend on a strongly-typed component crate.
